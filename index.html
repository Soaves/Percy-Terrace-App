Percy Terrace B&B

We are one of the closest bed and breakfast to town in the Alnwick area. We offer a high standard of comfortable, and inviting accommodation very near the centre of Alnwick. We have 2 large sunny double rooms, one with Ensuite and one Family Room with private facilities. Each room is decorated in an eye-catching style with colours that will attract and excite you. Original art on the walls completes an ambience not likely to be found elsewhere.

Accommodation is a three minute walk to the town square, Alnwick Garden, and Alnwick Castle. The breathtaking Northumbrian coastline is minutes away and offers miles of sandy coastline, ideal for walking.

We welcome walkers and cyclists where you are assured of a special welcome and access to local information to help you plan your excursions. We are within easy walking distance of all main attractions in Alnwick including the Alnwick Garden, Barter Books and the Playhouse. Please contact us either on +441665606867or by email.

Everything is individually prepared to order on the premises from a wide ranging menu. Special diets catered for including vegetarian and gluten free. In September we all enjoy our ever popular Food Festival and accompanying Beer Festival. Come and join us. Why not come and see this historic market town, home of the Duke and Duchess of Northumberland and many historic features. .

Terms and Conditions

For advanced bookings a minimum of two nights is usually required. Late bookings for single nights will be accepted if we have an available room but with an increased tariff of £5.00 per person.  Please Telephone us for availability. 

Reservations
A non-refundable deposit by debit card, credit card or cheque for the first night’s tariff is required to reserve each room. A charge of 2% is made for the use of credit cards to cover card processing cost.
Deposits should be received within 5 days of making the reservation otherwise we cannot guarantee the room. Deposit cheques should be made payable to Percy Terrace Bed and Breakfast.

Cancellations
If you cancel within 30 days of the first night's reservation the balance of the booking is payable. If we are able to re-let the room you would be refunded with deduction of an admin fee of £20 

Arrivals and Departures

Please arrive between 4pm and 9pm unless we have agreed and confirmed alternative arrangements.If unforeseen circumstances on the day affect your confirmed arrival schedule please telephone us.

Departure
Rooms should be vacated by 10.00 on your day of departure.

Settlement
Settlement by cash or debit card may be preferred as credit card payments carry a processing charge of up to 2% that would be added to the bill.

Dietary Requirements
If any guests have special dietary requirements we can cater for these if advised at the time of booking.

No Smoking
For the comfort and safety of all our guests Percy Terrace Bed and Breakfast is exclusively non- smoking. Abuse of this condition will incur a 100 pound surcharge fee to cover the cost of additional cleaning.
